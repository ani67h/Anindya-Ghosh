WELCOME TO ANINDYA'S UNIVERSE!

Hi, I am Anindya!

I’m a passionate and ambitious student from Bangladesh, currently in grade 8, studying under an international curriculum at Vertical Horizon. I have a deep love for science—especially astronomy, physics, and mathematics—which has led me to participate in academic competitions and Olympiads.

One of my proudest achievements was becoming the champion of the Bangladesh Astronomy Olympiad (BDAAO) 2024 regional round and advancing to the national level. I dream of becoming an aerospace engineer, and I’m always looking for ways to challenge myself in math and physics to move closer to that goal.

Beyond academics, I’m a creative and expressive person. I play the guitar, piano, and cajón, and music is a big part of who I am. I’ve also created a game on Roblox and run a personal project called Explorer’s Notebook, which allows me to combine learning with creativity.
